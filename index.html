Our Donation Plan

These are the ways how we distribute our collected funds among people.

Food for a family

Those who are suffering to manage the daily necessary food, we shall provide a package to sustain for a week.

Shelter for a family

Those who have lost their living places due to flood, we shall take them to our shelter center.

Disaster relief training

We provide our volunteers the necessary training to face the situation in the right way. In the training. they study case studies of previous disasters.
